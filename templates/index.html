FAQ

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Most online booking systems allow you to reschedule or cancel your appointment. However, there may be cancellation fees or specific timeframes within which changes are allowed. Check the terms and conditions of the service provider.

What if my bike needs additional repairs during the service?

If your bike needs additional repairs or maintenance beyond what was initially scheduled, the service center will typically contact you to discuss the required work and obtain your approval before proceeding.

How long will the service take?

The duration of the service depends on the type of service and the specific tasks involved. The service provider should be able to provide you with an estimated completion time when you book your appointment.

What should I do before taking my bike for service?

Before your service appointment, it's a good idea to clean your bike, remove any personal items, and ensure that you have all the necessary documents, such as your bike's registration and insurance details.

What if I face issues with the service quality?

If you are not satisfied with the service quality, contact the service center or the platform through which you booked the service. They should address your concerns and work to resolve any issues.
